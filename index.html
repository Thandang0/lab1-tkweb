 If this card is not equipped with an Equip Card, 
 you take no damage from battles involving this card.

 If either player equips an Equip Card(s) to this card: You can add 1 "Mikanko" Trap from your Deck to your hand.
 You can only use this effect of "Hu-Li the Jewel Mikanko" once per turn.
